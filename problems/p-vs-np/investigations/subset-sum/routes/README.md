- [Algorithmic landscape](../references/algorithmic-landscape.md) — organized comparison of currently known algorithmic, structural, compression, and barrier directions;
- [Primary-source map](../references/algorithmic-landscape-sources.md) — literature and project records supporting the landscape;

The landscape is an orientation artifact, not an active route and not a new mathematical claim.

## Route requirements

Every active route should contain:

- `README.md` — thesis, scope, and dependencies;
- `STATUS.md` — current operational state and next test;
- `CLAIMS.md` — route-local claim ledger;
- atomic directories for proofs, counterexamples, audits, experiments, notes, and archive.

A proposed route should also identify its corresponding landscape row or state explicitly why it is materially new.

Do not merge routes merely because they use related vocabulary. Preserve failed routes when they remain informative.
