## Final disposition

The route is closed as a broad structural-compression barrier route. It retains correct model-specific lower bounds and boundary theorems, but no model simultaneously subsumed the retained mechanisms, blocked polynomial Boolean simulation, and supported a superpolynomial hard-family lower bound. See [Closeout](CLOSEOUT.md).
