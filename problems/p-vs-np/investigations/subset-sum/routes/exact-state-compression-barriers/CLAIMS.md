## Route disposition

The route has established a precise model bracket. Unevaluated additive syntax is universally small, normalized progression unions can be exponentially large even on easy instances, and unrestricted intersection restores compact Boolean conjunction. The retained framework therefore requires a two-layer target-relative proof graph rather than a single final exact-set representation. A restricted arithmetic proof-graph candidate is now defined, but no lower bound or non-circuit-simulation theorem has been proved for it. Route identifiers use `SS-ECB-###`.
