## Final disposition

The route is closed as a path to a broad structural-compression barrier. It retains correct model-specific lower bounds and boundary theorems, but no model simultaneously subsumed the retained structural mechanisms, blocked polynomial Boolean simulation, and supported a superpolynomial hard-family lower bound. See [Closeout](CLOSEOUT.md).
