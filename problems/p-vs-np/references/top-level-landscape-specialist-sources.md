## Integer and convex optimisation

### `[P32]` Lenstra — integer programming in fixed dimension

Hendrik W. Lenstra Jr., “Integer Programming with a Fixed Number of Variables,” *Mathematics of Operations Research* 8(4), 1983, pp. 538–548.

- Role: polynomial-time solvability of integer programming when the dimension is fixed.
- Stable source: https://doi.org/10.1287/moor.8.4.538

### `[P33]` Goemans and Williamson — semidefinite relaxation for Max Cut

Michel X. Goemans and David P. Williamson, “Improved Approximation Algorithms for Maximum Cut and Satisfiability Problems Using Semidefinite Programming,” *Journal of the ACM* 42(6), 1995, pp. 1115–1145.

- Role: representative semidefinite-relaxation achievement and integrality-gap context for Max Cut.
- Stable source: https://doi.org/10.1145/227683.227684

## Arithmetic circuit complexity

### `[P35]` Shpilka and Yehudayoff — arithmetic circuit complexity survey

Amir Shpilka and Amir Yehudayoff, “Arithmetic Circuits: A Survey of Recent Results and Open Questions,” *Foundations and Trends in Theoretical Computer Science* 5(3–4), 2010, pp. 207–388.

- Role: arithmetic circuit models, polynomial identity testing, lower-bound techniques, and open barriers.
- Stable source: https://doi.org/10.1561/0400000039

## Descriptive complexity

### `[P36]` Immerman — descriptive complexity

Neil Immerman, *Descriptive Complexity*, Springer, 1999.

- Role: logical characterizations of complexity classes and lower bounds for restricted finite-model formalisms.
- Stable source: https://doi.org/10.1007/978-1-4612-0539-5

## Scope determination

These sources support only their stated specialist roles. They do not establish unrestricted polynomial-time algorithms, general Boolean circuit lower bounds, or a resolution of P versus NP.
